## Bio

Before uni, I mostly cared about sports and music. I practiced swimming, triathlon, surf, skate, volleyball... and played in a couple of bands: some rock/jazz as a drummer, some classical ensembles as a percussionist. Around the time I got into uni I also started making music on the computer, and soon I was working with other artists, mostly rappers: producing, recording in studios, mixing and mastering, as well as DJing. I ended up playing some pretty big festivals by chance.

I'm not that extroverted, so I drifted toward electronic music and audio-reactive / generative visuals (Blender, TouchDesigner, Resolume). There I experimented with GANs for image generation and started getting into computer vision. I was planning to do a master's and go into research, and around then transformers started really working.

I spent 2024 finishing my TFG and working with friends on random projects (one of which ended up involving a trip to Egypt), hackathons, ML competitions, and crypto adventures. In 2025 I started working at [Audialab](https://audialab.com/) and co-founded TriboSolutions; in September I joined Zepo too, and ended up juggling three things at once for a while. In 2026 I moved to Barcelona and met a lot of interesting people.
