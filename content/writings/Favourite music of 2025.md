For one more year, I will be compiling my favourite albums and EPs released this year. Also added a new section on live shows I saw. Items are listed in rought descending order of enjoyment.

[Here's the 2022 list](/data/2022.jpeg),  [[Favourite music of 2023]], [[Favourite music of 2024]].

Wrote a bit on [no-cosign](https://no-cosign.m19182.dev/), favourite ones were [Historia y Legado de Soulseek](https://no-cosign.m19182.dev/historia-y-legado-de-soulseek/) and [Las Herramientas Importan](https://no-cosign.m19182.dev/las-herramientas-importan/).

### Live shows

- Thundercat @ PulseOfGaia Festival
  - Extremely impressed by him live, one of the best shows I've seen in my life, he was shredding non stop and the energy was insane even thougt most people weren't there for him.
- aya + MFO @ MIRA Festival
  - aya had one of my favourite albums of the year and she delivered on the crazyness... MFO was doing laser visuals that were ok.
- Oscar Jerome @ Garufa
  - Took a bunch of friends with me and they all liked it, jazz-funk goodness.
- Amnesia Scanner + Fraxiom @ MIRA Festival
  - Hard sounds, great visuals, difficult to describe.
- JPEGMAFIA @ Sala Apolo
  - Even thought he is my most listened to artist for the second year in a row, live was a bit underwhelming, still fun though.
- LechugaZafiro and Verracon @ MIRA Festival
  - Surprising performance, I didn't expect much but they delivered hectic set that did not bring the energy down at any point, with fun internet coded visuals.
- Slipknot @ Resurrection Fest
  - I don't usually listen to metal, but this band live is quite something.
- Oneohtrix Point Never + Freeka Tet @ MIRA Festival
  - Played his new album live, honestly visuals the highlight of the show, Freeka Tet moved a character in a virtual world with textures and all kind of syncing.
- Blawan @ MIRA Festival
  - Had seen him DJ before but this time the sound system was way better and the set was actually live.
- Floating Points + Hamill Industries @ MIRA Festival
- Los Thuthanaka @ MIRA Festival
- Gutalax @ Resurrection Fest
  - This was something else... Grindcore from the Czech Republic.
- Gorillaz @ PulseOfGaia Festival
  - Good set, but was kind of annoyed by the crowd.
- Ali Sethi + Nicolas Jaar @ MIRA Festival
- John Mouse @ MIRA Festival
- Flying Lotus @ MIRA Festival
  - Holy shit this was bad, really dissapointed, visuals were cringe VJ Loops, music sounded bad, half of the set was shitty Ibiza house.

This is not an exhaustive list, just the ones that stood out the most to me.
